Credit Card Spending Analysis - Tableau + Excel Dashboard

This project analyzes 261 transactions totaling $7,987.22 in credit card spending throughout 2023.
The objective was to understand spending habits and trends to optimize budgeting strategies.

Top Spending Categories:
  • Online Shopping (30% of total spending)
  • Entertainment (21%)
  • Food Delivery (16%)
Spending Trends:
  • Monthly spending remained relatively consistent, with peaks in online shopping and food delivery categories during the summer months.
Credit Utilization:
  • The average credit utilization rate was 19.02%, reflecting a healthy credit usage relative to the card's limit.
Reccomendations:
  • Reduce Online Shopping: Accounts for 30% of expenses (Implement a budget to stay consistent)
  • Reduce Food Deliveries: Transitioning from food delivery to home-cooked meals could significantly reduce expenses.
  • Monitor Credit Utilization: Utilization below 20% is good, but reducing it to below 10% would be optimal to improve credit score
