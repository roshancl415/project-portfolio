Credit Card Spending Analysis

This project analyzes 261 transactions totaling $7,987.22 in 2023 credit card spending. The goal is to better understand spending habits to optimize budgeting strategies.

Interactive Tableau Dashboard

Key Insights

  • Top Categories: Online Shopping (30%), Entertainment (21%), Food Delivery (16%)
  • Spending Trends: Consistent monthly spend with peaks in shopping and entertainment during colder months
  • Credit Utilization: Avg. 19.02% is healthy, but reducing below 10% would boost scores even further

Recommendations

  • Cap online shopping with a fixed monthly budget
  • Explore more budget-friendly activities to enjoy during the colder months
  • Swap food delivery for home cooking to cut costs
  • Aim for under 10% utilization to maximize credit score
