SQL Server Database for Chase Center Stadium

The company that this database is designed for is GSW Arena, LLC. This company owns the Golden State Warriors Stadium known as “Chase Center” in San Francisco, California. This stadium operates year-round primarily acting as a home stadium for the Golden State Warriors basketball team. The purpose of creating this database is to ensure that GSW Arena LLC can easily access data relevant to analyzing, maintaining, and improving their overall stadium operations to ensure that maximum profit is being achieved.
